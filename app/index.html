Welcome to the first ever Women Who Code Hack Day in India!

This November our own Silicon City will experience some of the enthusiastic women developers coming together and hacking their way to glory.

Women Who Code is a group that supports women worldwide, no matter what their skills levels are, in learning about, practicing and exploring computer programming. All you need to have is a curious mind and passion to learn.

The theme for this meetup is “Hack A Way with AngularJS”. The 3-hours meetup is a great place for novice and experienced female developers to hone their AngularJS skills, meet other women from colleges and tech industry, network with their peers, and have a safe space to learn and ask questions.

There is just one mantra — “Work, Chat, Eat and Network”!

Have fun and happy coding!
